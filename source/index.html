Our Wines

Lake Roosevelt Wine Company produces world-class estate red wines with majestic Whitestone Rock in the horizon and the waters of Lake Roosevelt below.

The Vineyard

Situated along the beautiful shores of Lake Roosevelt, the area is an ideal place to grow French Bordeaux grape varietals, including Merlot and Cabernet Sauvignon.

Lake Roosevelt Wine Company

Situated along the beautiful shores of the mighty Lake Roosevelt is a legacy of grape growing that is testament to the unique, favorable soil and weather conditions naturally perfect for flavorful wines. With over a decade of experience growing wine grapes in the shadow of Whitestone Rock, LRWCo is proud to release wines exclusively showcasing this special area.
