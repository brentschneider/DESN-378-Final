Lake Roosevelt Wine Co.

Situated along the beautiful shores of the mighty Lake Roosevelt is a legacy of grape growing that is testament to the unique, favorable soil and weather conditions naturally perfect for growing French Bordeaux grape varietals, including Merlot, Cabernet Franc & Sauvignon.

Our winemaking philosophy begins in the vineyard, with over two decades of experience growing wine in the shadow of the majestic Whitestone Rock, Lake Roosevelt Wine Company is proud to release wines exclusively showcasing this special area. We do not buy fruit from any other source, our wines are 100% Estate grown.
